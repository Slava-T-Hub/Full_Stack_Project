Welcome to our social site for rare travels!

We are a community of passionate travelers united by our love for unique and off the beaten path destinations around the world. Our platform is designed for those who seek unforgettable and extraordinary adventures, eager to share their discoveries and be inspired by others' stories.

Here you will find: Stories and photo reports of rare and exotic routes. Useful tips and hacks from experienced travelers. Opportunities to find like minded people and organize group trips. Forums and chats for exchanging experiences and discussing exciting ideas.

Our mission is to create a space where everyone can share their experiences of rare travels, find new friends, and get inspired for new adventures. We believe that the most amazing places are often found off the tourist trails, and our goal is to help you discover them.

Join our community, share your stories, and explore the world from a fresh, unexpected perspective with us. Inspire and be inspired!
